## Generic strategic principles (name-free; safe to keep here)

Snapshot basis 2026-05-28 — re-verify with Luke / the private strategy doc if >4 weeks stale.

1. **Time-to-offer matters most right now.** There's a hard personal deadline (specifics in the private strategy doc). Bias toward fast-funnel channels — warm leads, roles already in process, recruiter inbound — over net-new cold-ATS volume.
2. **Targeting > volume on cold-ATS.** 3–5 high-conviction tailored applications per week beats 15 sprayed ones.
3. **Watch for re-listings of aspirational public-sector / civil-service roles** — they often cycle in 6–12 month windows.
4. **Rail-sector prospecting is a standing lens** (per [[user-profile]] rail thesis) — watch for relevant openings.
5. **Re-suggest interim / contract** as a runway-extender if the deadline approaches without a late-stage offer; don't re-raise unprompted before then.
