The `lukeblaney_cv_tailored/applications/` tracker should reflect entries where there is **active work** to do, not be treated as a complete record of every conversation Luke has ever had about a role.

**Why:** stated by Luke 2026-05-28 after I'd proposed backfilling ~8 historical network-channel leads "for completeness". His response: *"How useful is historical funnel data for you? I'd rather spend time focusing on future leads, rather than going over ones I didn't get."* With a tight runway, every hour spent on tracker bookkeeping is an hour not spent on outreach or interviews. Completeness has no payoff if it doesn't drive a different decision.

**How to apply:**

- **Capture an entry when there's active work on it.** Drafting a reach-back, prepping for an interview, deciding whether to chase, deciding whether to withdraw — these are real triggers. The tracker entry serves the work, not the other way round.
- **Don't backfill historical network leads** that died without engagement. If a buried lead resurfaces (someone gets back in touch after silence), add the entry then.
- **Cold-ATS applications stay captured by default** — the marginal cost is low (we're touching the tracker anyway when applying) and the cumulative pattern helps catch "stop wasting effort on this channel" signals.
- **One-off reconciliation passes are fine when they surface action**. Today's pre-rebuild outcome reconciliation was worth doing because it surfaced (a) the Wikimedia warm lead misfiled as a rejection and (b) confirmed which aspirational-set rejections are real. But that was a one-time review, not a routine.
- **Project-state framing has no payoff outside the tracker itself.** Don't create memory entries that just restate what's in `spotted.md` / `in-progress.md` / `closed.md` — point future-me at the live files instead.

**Edge case — when completeness DOES matter**: if Luke explicitly asks for a holistic funnel view ("show me everything in flight"), then comprehensive capture is the point. The default is action-driven; explicit holistic-view requests override.
