name: Don't suggest team shutdown
description: Stop asking user if they want to shut down the team — wait for instructions instead
type: feedback

Don't repeatedly ask the user whether to shut down the team. Idle teammates cost zero tokens. Just wait for the next instruction.

**Why:** The coordinator persona already says "Never shut down teammates unprompted" but the dispatcher was asking "shall I shut down?" at every quiet moment. This is noise — the user will say when they're done.

**How to apply:** After completing a task, report the outcome and stop. Don't append "anything else, or shall I shut down?" — just wait.
