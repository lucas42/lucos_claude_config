Label creation and management is the coordinator's responsibility — not `lucos-system-administrator` or other personas.

**Why:** The coordinator owns the label taxonomy and conventions. Other personas may create labels ad-hoc without following the standard set.

**How to apply:** Any time a missing label is discovered or labels need to be added to a repo, the coordinator handles it directly (no delegation needed since the role is now merged into team-lead).
